Your phone is reporting to Sky Map that its internal compass is uncalibrated. This is not a Sky Map issue, but we can help you try to fix it.

To calibrate, move your phone as follows:

  • Tilt your phone forward and back
  • Move it side to side
  • And then tilt left and right
Repeat until the compass accuracy is medium or high. If this does not work try the "figure of 8" method (video).
